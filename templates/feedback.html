Help Improve Our Search Results

Your feedback helps us improve the relevance of our semantic search engine. You'll be shown search results for predefined queries and asked to evaluate their relevance.

What you'll be doing:

  • You'll see search results for {{ total_queries }} different queries
  • For each result, you'll be asked: "Is this result relevant to the query?"
  • Click "Relevant" if the artwork matches the query
  • Click "Skip" if the artwork is not relevant
  • You can stop the process at any time

⚠️ Important Information:

  • This process may take 10-15 minutes to complete
  • You'll evaluate approximately 50-100 search results
  • Your feedback will be saved anonymously
  • You can stop and resume later if needed
